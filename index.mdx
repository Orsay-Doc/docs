Welcome. Orsay is an **AI sales rep that runs on personal WhatsApp, WhatsApp Business, and Instagram inbound** — it qualifies leads, replies in your tone of voice, and books calls while you sleep.

These docs are organized **by channel** so you can pick yours and follow the four-step setup: **connect → agent → sequence → conversations**.
