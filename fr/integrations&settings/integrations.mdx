To go to this menu, click on **“integrations”** in the bottom left-hand corner.

Click on **“browse integrations”**

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_integration/1.png" />
## To connect “WhatsApp”:

- Click on **“WhatsApp”**, **“Connect WhatsApp”**
- Then on **“Create WhatsApp profile”**.
- Fill in your details, click on **“confirm and continue”**.
- Click on **“Connect WhatsApp number”**.
- Once back in [app.orsay.ai](http://app.orsay.ai/), click on **“create inbound message”**.
- Put in the message you want and click on **“finish and create link”**.

Your WhatsApp profile is successfully integrated

## If you wish to modify :

- Click on the **“...”** next to the organization in question
- Then click on **“Edit”**.
- Modify, delete as required

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_integration/8.png" />

## To delete something:

- Click on the **“...”** next to the organization in question
- Select **“delete"**
- Then click on **“remove agent”**.

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_integration/9.png" />

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_integration/10.png" />
## If you need to delete a number :

**In the organization :**

- Go to **“link phone number"**

## Upload files :

- Import CSV file
- Fill in the country **“france”** (if French)
- First name/phone number : fill in the column title with the information
- Select the right sequence
- Click on **“upload file”**

To upload a CSV you need to have a sequence with trigger type <code style={{ color: '#d00' }}>CONTACT_SUBSCRIBED_TO_SEQUENCE</code>
