---
title: "4. Connect Zapier to Orsay"
description: "Pipe leads from Facebook Ads, landing pages, or any source straight into your WhatsApp Business sequence."
---

If your leads come from outside Orsay — Facebook Ads, ClickFunnels, systeme.io, your CRM — connect **Zapier** so every new lead automatically flows into your WhatsApp Business sequence and gets the approved template message within minutes.

## How it works

A Zap watches your lead source. When a new lead is captured, Zapier calls Orsay's **Create Lead for Sequence** action, which drops the lead into the sequence you choose. The sequence's approved template goes out, and the agent takes over after the lead replies.

## Set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Zapier in Orsay">
    Go to **Integrations** → click **Connect Zapier**.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_zapier/1.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new Zap">
    In Zapier, click **+ Create** to start a new Zap.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_zapier/2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your lead source">
    Choose the platform that captures your leads — **Facebook Lead Ads**, **ClickFunnels**, **systeme.io**, or any compatible source.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_zapier/3.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the Orsay action">
    Select **Create Lead for Sequence** so each lead lands directly into a specific WhatsApp Business sequence.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_zapier/4.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map fields">
    Map the lead's **First Name**, **Last Name**, and **Phone Number** from your source to Orsay's fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick organization and sequence">
    Select the **Organization** and the **WhatsApp Business sequence** the lead should enter.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_zapier/6.png" />

    <Tip>
    To find a sequence ID, click next to the sequence name on the Sequences page.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_zapier/5.png" />
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn the Zap on">
    Once the Zap is live, every new lead captured by your source flows into Orsay and triggers your WhatsApp Business sequence with the approved template message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sequence trigger requirement

The WhatsApp Business sequence you point Zapier at must use the **Contact subscribed to sequence** trigger. This trigger fires every time Orsay receives a new lead via Zapier (or CSV import).
