The **agent** is the AI brain that replies to every lead. Personal WhatsApp uses the same agent system as every other channel — once built, an agent can power any sequence.

## Quick path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agents">
    In the left sidebar, click **[Agents](https://app.orsay.ai/agents)** → **+ Create agent**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Guided AI Setup">
    The fastest start. Answer 8 short questions about your business, your offer, and your tone. Orsay generates the prompt for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Click **Save draft**, preview the conversation in the test panel, then **Publish agent**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

→ Full walkthrough: [Agents — full guide](/en/guides&tutorials/agents)
