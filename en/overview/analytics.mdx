A metrics dashboard for conversation performance.

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_overview/analytics.png" />

- Date range picker (defaults to today)
- Sequence filter to drill into specific campaigns
- Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds
- Metrics cards: Total Leads, Total Responded, Positive / Negative / Engaged / No Answer counts and percentages, Response Rate, Positive Rate

## Export your analytics [#export]

You can export everything visible on the Analytics dashboard with a single click.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Analytics">
    Go to **[Analytics](https://app.orsay.ai/analytics)** and apply any filters you want (date range, sequence).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export">
    Click the **Export** button at the top right of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the CSV">
    A CSV containing all metrics for the current filter range is downloaded to your computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
