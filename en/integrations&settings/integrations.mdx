## To connect “WhatsApp”:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connect WhatsApp">
    - Click on **“WhatsApp”**, **“Connect WhatsApp”**
    - Then on **“Create WhatsApp profile”**
    - Fill in your details, click on **“Confirm and continue”**
    - Click on **“Connect WhatsApp number”**
    - Once back in [app.orsay.ai](http://app.orsay.ai/), click on **“Create inbound message”**
    - Enter the message you want and click on **“Finish and create link”**

Your WhatsApp profile is successfully integrated.

## Upload files:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Upload">
    - Import CSV file
    - Fill in the country **“France”** (if French)
    - First name/phone number: fill in the column title with the information
    - Select the right sequence
    - Click on **“Upload file”**

To upload a CSV, you need to have a sequence with trigger type <code style={{ color: '#d00' }}>CONTACT_SUBSCRIBED_TO_SEQUENCE</code>
