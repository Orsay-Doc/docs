## Voice notes in personal WhatsApp sequences [#personal-whatsapp-audio]

<sub>*Released April 2026* · `personal-whatsapp` · `audio`</sub>

Connect your **personal WhatsApp** with a QR code — no Business API setup — and **send a voice note as the template (first message)** of any sequence. Leads hear your real voice before reading any text, which dramatically lifts reply rate on cold outreach.

- Connect via **Integrations → Personal WhatsApp** → scan the QR code on your phone.
- In any Personal WhatsApp sequence, switch the **first action step** to *Voice note* — record in-editor or upload (max 25 MB; MP3, M4A, WAV, OGG, AAC, WebM).
- The voice note plays *before* the text; subsequent steps stay in text and delays.

**→ [Full guide: Personal WhatsApp + voice notes](/en/guides&tutorials/sequences#personal-whatsapp-audio)**

## Claude MCP integration [#claude-mcp]

Use Orsay's tools — sequences, conversations, lead lookups — directly from **Claude** (or any MCP-compatible client) with one-click MCP setup. Pipe Orsay into your existing AI workflow without writing code.

## Voice cloning [#voice-cloning]

<sub>*Released March 2026* · `voice` · `tts`</sub>

Clone your voice from a 1-2 minute sample and send **personalized AI voice messages** to leads — at scale, in your own voice, with three tone presets.

- Record or upload a sample → cloned voice ready in minutes.
- Three tones: *Professional*, *Friendly*, *Enthusiastic*.
- Up to **5 cloned voices** per organization.
