Every Instagram lead that interacts appears in **Conversations**.

## Follow conversations in Orsay

The Conversations page lists every active thread (DM, story reply, or comment-converted-to-DM), classified by the AI:

- **Positive** — booked or about to book
- **Engaged** — talking but not qualified yet
- **Negative** — not interested
- **Undetermined** — no signal yet

Each thread shows the trigger (DM / Story reply / Comment) and links to the original post or story. Filter, sort, and search across everything.

## Take over a conversation

For high-value leads, step in yourself:

- Open the thread and reply directly in Orsay.
- Send text, an image, or a voice note from the input bar.
- Use **Text-to-Audio** to send a voice note in your cloned voice. → [Voice cloning](/en/guides&tutorials/voice-cloning)

When you reply, the AI agent **disables itself for that thread automatically**. Re-enable it with the toggle when you're done.

<Note>
Instagram messages are not synced to your phone via Orsay. All takeover happens inside the Conversations page (or directly inside the Instagram app, if you prefer — but Orsay won't know you replied there until the next message arrives).
</Note>

## Re-classify and improve the agent

If the AI puts a thread in the wrong bucket, re-classify it manually. Your corrections feed [Conversation Improvements](/en/overview/conversation-improvements), where the AI scans the last 24h of conversations and suggests rules to add to your agents.
