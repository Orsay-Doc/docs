The **agent** is the AI that replies to every incoming Instagram interaction. Same agent system as every channel.

## Quick path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agents → + Create agent">
    Open **[Agents](https://app.orsay.ai/agents)** and click **+ Create agent**. Use **Guided AI Setup** for the fastest start. Answer 8 short questions and Orsay writes the prompt for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Preview in the test panel, then **Publish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

→ Full walkthrough: [Agents — full guide](/en/guides&tutorials/agents)
