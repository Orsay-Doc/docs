Orsay AI automates Instagram lead generation by proactively finding and engaging qualified prospects from your competitors' audiences. The AI reviews profiles, filters qualified leads, and sends them a DM — all within Instagram's safe limits.

---

## Step 1 — Connect your Instagram account

1. Go to **Integrations** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Instagram (Lead Generation)** then **Manage**
3. Enter your Instagram credentials:
   - **Account:** Select your Instagram account
   - **Password:** Your Instagram password
   - **Secret Key:** Follow the video below

### Warm-up process

To keep your account safe, Orsay runs a comprehensive warm-up:

- **Warm-up period (without DMs): 3-5 days**
- **Ramp-up phase (from 5 DMs/day to 80 DMs/day): ~3 weeks**

During this process, you may receive an email asking you to reconnect your Instagram account. This is normal — just message our support team so we can help you reconnect smoothly.

---

## Step 2 — Create an outbound sequence

1. Go to **Sequences** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Create Sequence**
3. Select **Instagram** (left side)
4. Select an **Outbound** trigger:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="New Follower (your account)">
    DMs your most recent followers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Followers (of another account)">
    Contacts the most recent followers of other Instagram accounts. Enter target accounts by **@username** or **username** — you can add multiple accounts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="New Likes (specific post)">
    DMs people who like one of your posts (you can choose multiple). Most recent likes are prioritized. Enter the post URLs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Comments (on another account)">
    DMs people who comment on your competitors' posts (you can choose multiple). Most recent comments are prioritized. Enter the post URLs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Add actions to your sequence

Click the **"+"** button to add action steps. Actions are executed sequentially when a trigger fires.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Follow an account">

    - Follow accounts (up to **75 per day**)
    - Toggle **auto-unfollow** on the right and set how long you want to stay followed

  <Accordion title="Like posts">

    - Click Continue
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Send a message">
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/4.0.png" />
    - Send a message (up to **40 per day**)

    - Enter the message you want to send
    - Click Continue
  </Accordion>

<Tip>
Add a delay between each action to make your outreach look more natural to prospects. Our recommendation: Follow first, then like a post, then send a message.
</Tip>

### Configure your sequence settings

- **Select the account** you want to interact from (this is where your follow, like, and message actions will come from)

#### If you're sending DMs (optional):

- **(Optional) Create the template message** your AI agent will send. The agent takes over once the lead responds.
  - Use **&#123;&#123;name&#125;&#125;** to automatically insert the lead's first name
- **Filtering Agent:** Describe your ideal customer profile
- **Daily limit:** How many DMs per day (max 50 once ramp-up is finished)
- **Agent:** Select which AI agent handles the conversation
- **Contact people with existing conversation history:** Choose YES or NO
- **Answer delay:** Add a delay before the agent responds to make it feel natural
