Orsay AI captures incoming leads with instant DM responses and automated follow-ups that convert your Instagram community into booked appointments. This works for people who **comment on your posts**, **reply to your stories**, or **send you a DM**.

<Info>
Inbound sequences have **no daily limits** — unlike outbound, you can handle unlimited incoming interactions.
</Info>

---

## Step 1 — Connect your Instagram account

1. Go to **Integrations** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Instagram** → **Connect Instagram**
3. Follow the connection flow to link your Instagram account

This is the standard Instagram connection (not Lead Generation) — it allows Orsay to read and respond to your DMs, story replies, and comments.

---

## Step 2 — Create your AI agent

Before setting up your sequence, create an AI agent that will handle conversations with incoming leads.

[See how to create an AI agent](/en/guides&tutorials/agents)

---

## Step 3 — Create an inbound sequence

Go to **Sequences** → **Create Sequence** → Select **Instagram** → Choose an **Inbound** trigger.

You can create separate sequences for each trigger type:

### Message received

Triggers when someone sends you a direct message.

<Expandable title="Setup steps">
  1. Click **+** → **Add action step**
  2. **Select your Instagram account**
  3. **Select your AI agent**
  4. **Contact people with existing conversation history:** Choose **YES** to respond to everyone, or **NO** to only respond to new people you haven't interacted with before
  5. **Answer delay:** Add a delay before the agent responds to make it feel natural
  6. **Follow-ups:** Automatically follow up with leads who've engaged but gone quiet
  7. Click **Confirm**
</Expandable>

---

### Story reply

Triggers when someone replies to one of your Instagram stories.

<Expandable title="Setup steps">
  1. Click **+** → **Add action step**
  2. **Select your Instagram account**

  3. **(Optional) Add trigger keywords** to make this a **keyword-specific sequence**:
     - The sequence will only trigger when the story reply contains one of those keywords
     - If no keywords are added, it becomes a **global sequence** that triggers on all story replies
     - Keywords are **case-insensitive** and use **OR logic** (any keyword match triggers it)

  4. **(Optional) Select target stories** to limit the sequence to replies on specific active stories only. If no stories are selected, it triggers for all stories.

  5. **Write your template message** — this is the first message sent to the person. You can include a **link** or a **button** in this message.

  6. **Select your AI agent**
  7. **Contact people with existing conversation history:** Choose **YES** or **NO** to control whether you respond to people you've already interacted with
  8. **Answer delay** and **Follow-ups** settings
  9. Click **Confirm**
</Expandable>

---

### Comment on your posts

Triggers when someone comments on one of your Instagram posts.

<Expandable title="Setup steps">
  1. Click **+** → **Add action step**
  2. **Select your Instagram account**

  3. **(Optional) Add trigger keywords** to make this a **keyword-specific sequence**:
     - The sequence will only trigger when the comment contains one of those keywords
     - If no keywords are added, it becomes a **global sequence** that triggers on all comments
     - Keywords are **case-insensitive** and use **OR logic**

  4. **(Optional) Select target posts** to limit the sequence to comments on specific posts only. If no posts are selected, it triggers for all posts.

  5. **Write your template message** — this is the DM sent to the person who commented. You can include a **link** or a **button** in the message.

  6. **Select your AI agent**
  7. **Contact people with existing conversation history:** Choose **YES** or **NO**
  8. **Answer delay** and **Follow-ups** settings
  9. Click **Confirm**
</Expandable>

## How keywords and targeting work together

You can combine keywords and post/story targeting to create highly specific sequences:

| Sequence Type | Keywords | Target Posts/Stories | Behavior |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Global** | None | None | Triggers on all comments/replies (fallback) |
| **Keyword-specific** | Set | None | Triggers only when text contains a keyword |
| **Post/Story-scoped** | Optional | Set | Triggers only for selected posts/stories |

<Info>
**Matching priority:** When a comment or story reply arrives, sequences are evaluated in this order:
1. **Post/Story-scoped sequences** — checked first
2. **Keyword-specific sequences** — checked next
3. **Global sequence** — used as fallback
</Info>

<Tip>
**Example:** Create a keyword sequence with "price" and "pricing" to route pricing inquiries to a specialized agent, while a global sequence handles all other comments with a general-purpose agent.
</Tip>
