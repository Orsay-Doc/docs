In the **“Sequences”** section, you can create and manage the process that will enable you to send, delay, and schedule messages, as well as manage the AI agent.

- Go to your organization in the top left-hand corner
- Click on “Sequences"
- Then click on “Create Sequence”

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_s/1.png" />

The “Create your sequence” page will appear :

Select your support

## 📸 Instagram

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create Instagram Inbounds sequences">
  
    - Select inbounds triggers and click on “+” and “add action step”.
  
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_s_i_i/1.png" />
  
    - Select your Instagram account and click on “continue”.
  
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_s_i_i/2.png" />
  
    Only for **"Story reply"** and **"Comment"**:

    - **(Optional) Add trigger keywords** to create a **specific sequence**. The sequence will only trigger when the comment or story reply contains one of those keywords. If no keywords are added, it becomes a **global sequence** that triggers on every incoming comment or story reply. Keywords are case-insensitive and matched with OR logic.

    - **(Optional) Select target posts or stories** to scope the sequence to specific content. If no posts/stories are selected, the sequence applies to all of them.

    <Tip>
    **Matching priority:** Post/story-scoped sequences are checked first, then keyword-specific sequences, then the global (no-keyword) sequence as a fallback.
    </Tip>

    - Create your template message and click on "continue".

    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_s_i_i/3.png" />

    - Select agent, answer delay and click on "continue"

    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_s_i_i/4.png" />

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Create Instagram Outbounds sequences">
    Setting Up Triggers and Actions  
  Start by selecting a trigger such as “New Followers” or “New Like.”  
  Then add the actions that will be executed from your Instagram account.  
  All actions added to the same flow will be triggered sequentially on this account when the trigger is activated.

Example:

Trigger: “New Like” (on a specific post).  
 Actions: “Follow” then “Like.”  
 Result: If someone likes the post, the system will first follow that user, then like one of their posts.

    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/2.1.png" />
    - Add the source: use @username for followers and the post URL
    for likes or comments.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/3.1.png" />
    - Define the target prospect profile, then click on "Continue".
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/4.1.png" />
    - Click on the "+", then on "Add action step".
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/1.png" />
    - Choose the type of action you want to perform, then click on "Continue".
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/2.png" />
    - It is possible to combine several actions to perform multiple steps within the same sequence.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/6.png" />
    - You can also add a delay between each action.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/3.png" />
    - And choose the duration, either in hours or days.
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_istg/4.png" />
    <Tip>
      Our recommandations:
      - 1.⁠ ⁠Follow
      - 2.⁠ ⁠like a post
      - 3.⁠ Send a message.
    </Tip>

  </Accordion>
