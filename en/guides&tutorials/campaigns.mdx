The **Campaigns** feature lets you send a personalized message to multiple leads at once — perfect for announcements, promotions, or re-engagement.

## Getting started

- Click on **"Campaigns"** in the left sidebar
- Select the channels and lead statuses you want to target
- Write your message, then send

## Step 1 — Select your filters

Choose which leads to reach by selecting:

- **Channels**: Instagram, WhatsApp, or both
- **Lead classification**: Positive, Negative, Engaged, Undetermined, or all

Only leads matching **all** selected filters will appear in the list.

## Step 2 — Write your message

Type your broadcast message in the text area. You can personalize it using variables:

| Variable | Replaced by |
|---|---|
| `{{firstname}}` | Lead's first name |
| `{{lastname}}` | Lead's last name |
| `{{username}}` | Lead's Instagram/WhatsApp username |

Click the variable buttons below the text area to insert them directly.

<Tip>
  A live preview is shown on the right so you can see exactly what a lead will receive.
</Tip>

## Step 3 — Select or exclude leads

Two modes are available:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Send to all (default)">
    By default, the campaign targets **all matching leads**. You can exclude specific leads by unchecking them in the list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pick manually">
    Switch to manual mode to hand-pick exactly which leads should receive the message. Use the search bar and "Select all" / "Deselect all" buttons to speed things up.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 4 — Send

Click the **"Send to X lead(s)"** button. You'll see a confirmation toast with:

- How many leads received the message
- How many leads were **skipped** (if any)

## Messaging window limits

Meta enforces messaging windows on both channels. Leads whose last inbound message is too old will be automatically skipped:

| Channel | Window | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **WhatsApp** | 24 hours | Free-form text is only allowed within 24h of the lead's last message. Outside this window, only pre-approved template messages can be sent. |
| **Instagram** | 24 hours (7 days with HUMAN_AGENT approval) | Instagram's standard messaging window is 24h. If your Meta app has the `human_agent` permission approved, this extends to 7 days. |

<Warning>
  Leads outside the messaging window are silently skipped — they will **not** receive the message and will not appear as failed. The skip count is shown in the confirmation toast.
</Warning>

## Best practices

- **Test with a small group first** — send to 2-3 leads manually before broadcasting to everyone.
- **Keep messages short and personal** — use `{{firstname}}` to avoid generic-sounding blasts.
- **Avoid external links on Instagram** — Instagram may flag or suppress messages containing URLs sent via the API.
- **Check your lead list** — make sure the filters match the audience you intend to reach.
