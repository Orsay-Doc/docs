The **Instagram Lead Finder** lets you find your most qualified prospects directly from Orsay — **no need to connect your Instagram account**.

Our AI scans every day the people who interact with Instagram accounts or posts of your choice, scores them against your criteria, and presents you with a ranked table of your most relevant leads.

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.75rem", marginTop: "1rem" }} src="/image_lead_finder/1.png" />

---

## Step 1 — Define your triggers

Triggers determine which people enter your lead pipeline. Two trigger types are available:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="New followers" icon="user-plus">
    Anyone who starts following **any Instagram account** you are targeting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="New likes" icon="heart">
    Anyone who likes **any Instagram post** you are targeting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

You can combine multiple triggers and multiple sources (accounts or posts) within a single Lead Finder.

---

## Step 2 — Daily AI analysis

Every day, Orsay's AI automatically scans the new people who have activated your triggers.

<Info>
  No manual action is needed on your end. The Lead Finder runs continuously and feeds your prospect table over time.
</Info>

---

## Step 3 — Set your filter

Define your ideal prospect profile by entering a filter in plain language.

**Filter examples:**
- `entrepreneur, male, under 40`
- `fitness coach, female, United States`
- `startup founder, tech industry`

The AI will use this filter to evaluate each detected profile and assign it a relevance score.

---

## Step 4 — Relevance score

For each new profile detected, the AI visits the person's Instagram page and assigns a **relevance score from 1 to 5**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Profile analysis">
    The AI reviews the bio, posts, follower count, and all available signals on the public Instagram profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Comparison with your filter">
    The profile is compared against your filter to measure how well it matches your ideal prospect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score assignment">
    A score of **1 to 5** is assigned: the higher the score, the more qualified the lead according to your criteria.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 5 — Qualified leads table

Check your leads table to get an at-a-glance view of all your prospects ranked by score.

The table shows for each lead:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Profile** | Name and direct link to the Instagram profile |
| **Trigger** | The action that brought the lead in (new follower / new like) |
| **Score** | Relevance score from 1 to 5 assigned by the AI |
| **Source** | The Instagram account or post being monitored |

---

## Step 6 — Automate interactions

Once your leads are identified, you can directly **automate interactions** from your Instagram account using Orsay sequences.

<Tip>
  From the leads table, launch an outbound sequence to automatically send a message, a follow, or a like to your highest-scored prospects.
</Tip>

See the [Sequences](/en/guides&tutorials/sequences) page to configure your Instagram automations.
