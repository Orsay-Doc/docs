**The menu is at the top left.**

This menu will be used to manage your conversations, lead qualifications, send messages, and files.

To view your conversations, you need to create your [sequence](/en/guides&tutorials/sequences) first.

## If the sequence is created correctly

The page will be displayed like this:

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_conversations/1.png" />

If you wish to sort your conversations:

- Click on **“newest”**
- Here you can choose to sort between **“newest”** and **“oldest”**
  <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_conversations/2.png" />

If you wish to sort your conversations by lead type:

Click on **“show:”**

Here you can choose between:

- **positive**
- **engaged**
- **indeterminate**
- **negative**

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_conversations/3.png" />

You can also re-classify conversations manually. To do this:

- Click on the arrow (engaged, positive, etc.)
- Select the state of the conversation
- Click on **“Confirm”**

The conversation will be placed directly in the correct section.

<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_conversations/4.png" />
<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_conversations/5.png" />
<img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/image_conversations/6.png" />
