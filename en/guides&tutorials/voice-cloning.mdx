The **voice cloning** feature lets you create a digital copy of your voice, then generate personalized audio messages directly from your conversations. Your leads will receive voice notes indistinguishable from real ones.

## Clone your voice

To clone your voice, go to **Integrations → Voice Cloning**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Record audio samples">
    Record yourself via the microphone or upload existing audio files. The more varied and high-quality your samples, the more accurate the clone.

    - Up to **25 audio files**
    - Maximum **25 MB** total
    - Maximum **5 voices** per organization
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your voice">
    Give your voice a name (e.g. "My pro voice", "Enthusiastic voice").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the clone">
    Click **"Clone"**. The voice is created in seconds and immediately available in your conversations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate a voice message

From any conversation, click the **audio** icon to open the voice message generator.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a voice">
    Choose from your cloned voices or the default voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your message">
    Type the text you want to convert to audio. The text will be automatically optimized for natural speech delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the tone">
    Three tones are available:

    - **Professional** — stable and composed
    - **Friendly** — casual and warm
    - **Enthusiastic** — dynamic and energetic
  </Step>
  <Step title="Advanced options (optional)">
    - **Natural hesitations** — Add filler words (um, well, you know…) for an even more human feel. You can customize the word list.
    - **Background noise** — Add ambient sound (café or street) to make it sound like the message was recorded in a real setting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and send">
    Preview the audio, then click **"Use this audio"** to attach it to your WhatsApp message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Natural hesitations

To make your voice messages even more realistic, enable **natural hesitations**. The AI will inject filler words and self-corrections into your text before synthesis.

Default words: `um, well, so, like, you know, I mean`

<Tip>
  You can edit the hesitation word list directly in the text field. Adapt them to your speaking style!
</Tip>

## Background noise

Two ambient sounds are available:

| Ambiance | Description |
|---|---|
| ☕ **Café** | Coffee shop background — distant conversations, cups, warm atmosphere |
| 🚗 **Street** | Traffic noise — cars, urban atmosphere |

The noise is mixed at a subtle volume so it doesn't overpower your voice.

## Manage your voices

From **Integrations → Voice Cloning**, you can:

- View all your cloned voices
- Listen to a preview of each voice
- Delete a voice

<Warning>
  Deleting a voice is permanent. Previously generated audio messages remain available, but you won't be able to create new messages with that voice.
</Warning>
